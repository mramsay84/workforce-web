{/* ============================================================
  BLOCK 2: THE FOUR STEPS
  Source: website-content-blueprint.md §2.2 | sitemap-and-page-architecture.md §3.2 Block 2
  Required elements (each step: heading + paragraph + visual/diagram):
    Step 1 — "You brief the platform"
      - What you provide: description of work, output needed, company rules
      - What you DON'T need to provide: role definitions, task assignments, coordination logic
    Step 2 — "A planner designs your team"
      - Planner interviews you / reads your brief, selects work mode, proposes team composition
      - Proposal submitted for YOUR approval before any work starts
      - You see: plan, team structure, execution sequence — you approve or refine
    Step 3 — "The team executes in parallel"
      - Leads own domains, hire specialists
      - Specialists work simultaneously (not sequentially)
      - Inter-agent communication via structured protocol
      - Review cycles enforced: no specialist work closes without lead sign-off
    Step 4 — "You review what matters"
      - You receive the completed deliverable
      - High-stakes decisions come back as proposals during execution
      - You do NOT manage tasks, review drafts, or babysit the process
  Constraints:
    - Each step names the role (Planner, Leads, Specialists) explicitly
    - Parallel execution must be clearly distinguished from sequential tools
============================================================ */}
