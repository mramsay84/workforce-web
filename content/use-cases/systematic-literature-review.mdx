<Section title="What agents produce">

A structured literature review with evidence grading, PRISMA-style methodology documentation, synthesis of findings across sources, and a hypothesis tracking log showing supporting and contradicting evidence for each claim. Every source is graded; every claim is traceable.

</Section>

<Section title="How it runs" variant="surface">
  <StepList steps={[
    {
      title: "Research methodology lead designs the approach",
      description: "Defines search strategy, inclusion/exclusion criteria, evidence grading standards, and the scope of each specialist's coverage."
    },
    {
      title: "Domain specialists research in parallel",
      description: "Each specialist covers a different sub-topic or body of literature simultaneously. One on methodology, one on outcomes, one on comparative studies."
    },
    {
      title: "Critique phase challenges the evidence",
      description: "Agents review each other's source quality before synthesis. A specialist challenging another's use of a secondary source — before it goes into the final document."
    },
    {
      title: "Synthesis specialist integrates findings",
      description: "One agent responsible for integrating all research into a coherent, consistently voiced literature review. No stitched-together outputs."
    }
  ]} />
</Section>
