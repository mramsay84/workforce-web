<Section title="What agents produce">

A complete investment memo in standard format — market analysis, competitive landscape, company deep-dive, financial model review, risk assessment, and executive summary. Three agent teams work simultaneously: market research, financial analysis, and legal review. A synthesis lead integrates findings into the final memo.

</Section>

<Section title="How it runs" variant="surface">
  <StepList steps={[
    {
      title: "Market research team analyses the landscape",
      description: "Research agents map the competitive landscape, size the market, and analyse comparable transactions — in parallel, with evidence grading."
    },
    {
      title: "Financial team reviews the model",
      description: "Financial analysis agents validate assumptions, stress-test unit economics, run sensitivity analyses, and benchmark against comparable companies."
    },
    {
      title: "Legal team flags the risks",
      description: "Legal review agents assess corporate structure, cap table, IP ownership, regulatory exposure, and management risk. RED findings always escalate."
    },
    {
      title: "Synthesis lead assembles the memo",
      description: "A dedicated synthesis agent integrates findings from all three teams into a coherent investment memo — one voice, consistent analysis."
    }
  ]} />
</Section>
