<Section title="What agents produce">

A triage report for each NDA: GREEN (standard, sign), YELLOW (negotiate), RED (escalate). Clause-by-clause redline on non-standard terms. Escalation flags for items requiring human legal review. The full batch — not one at a time.

</Section>
